Mamet Poster
    
 

I designed this poster for a student theater group at Princeton. They were performing two one act plays by David Mamet. The actual size was 14" X 23" and it was printed on matte white paper, with a glossy varnish over the black ink.

Some of the cast wanted a big fat duck on the poster. I thought that was too obvious, but a duck seems to have crept in unconsciously anyway. The type treatment of “Duck Variations” resembles an open beak pointing right, or a duck head and body looking left. Some people also see a feather in the skewed type.
