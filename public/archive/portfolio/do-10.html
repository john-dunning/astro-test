Digital Objectives
    
 

Another prototype that I created for Digital Objectives was an electronic version of the Nursing94 Drug Handbook. This volume is widely used by nurses and other medical personnel, but its heft makes it inconvenient to carry. Thus it was a perfect candidate for translation into the Newton electronic book format.

I mocked up one of its chapters, add-ing hypertext and search capabilities. The main design consideration was closely emulating the printed book’s format, both to ease the nurses’ transition from paper to PDA and to demonstrate to the publisher that the Newton is a viable content delivery platform.

They were very excited about the possibilities, but the head of their project was unfortunately laid off. The demo did, however, help to convince Mosby, another large medical publisher, to contract Digital Objectives to produce Newton versions of some of its publications.
