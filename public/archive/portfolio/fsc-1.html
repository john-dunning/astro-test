Foundation for Student Communication
    
 

For a couple of years I was the art director for a student organization called the Foundation for Student Communication. Three times a year FSC publishes Business Today magazine, a four-color glossy which is mailed to 200,000 juniors and seniors at 100 colleges across the country.

Besides producing Business Today, FSC also holds annual national and international Business Tomorrow conferences, which bring together students and business leaders to discuss a particular issue.

This booklet, published after the 1990 international conference, was used in fund-raising efforts during the summer to show what the conferences are all about. I designed the conference logos and the overall grid system for the booklets, and also laid out most of the international booklet.

As an historical note, the logo was created with Illustrator 88. This was back in the dark ages when Illustrator was unable to kern letter pairs. So to adjust the spacing I had to break up the type into separate blocks.
